# Senior Backend Engineer — Fintech Payments

Domain-depth happy path. Payments is a specific enough domain that
generic backend candidates shouldn't rank as high as ones with actual
payments experience. Good demo of hybrid RAG retrieval surfacing
domain expertise.

Expected behavior: top pick around 0.75+ with strong domain match
(usually a candidate with explicit payment-systems experience).

---

**Title**
Senior Backend Engineer — Fintech Payments

**Description**
Senior backend engineer to build and operate payment processing
services for a global fintech platform. You will design idempotent
payment APIs, integrate with card networks and bank rails, handle
reconciliation and dispute flows, and ensure PCI-DSS and SOC 2
compliance throughout. Low-latency gRPC services with Postgres and
Redis. You will collaborate with risk engineering on fraud-detection
signals and with platform on observability.

**Must-have skills**
Python, PostgreSQL, gRPC, Payment systems

**Nice-to-have skills**
Redis, Kafka, PCI-DSS, SOC 2, Fraud detection

**Years of experience (min – max)**
5 – 12

**Target hiring date**
2026-11-01

**Location**
Bangalore, India

**Remote OK?**
Yes

**Employment type**
full_time
